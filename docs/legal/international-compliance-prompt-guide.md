
# International Compliance Documents Prompt Guide for Doc Legal AI

This document provides structured prompts for generating comprehensive international compliance documents for Ascended Social's global operations, particularly focusing on GDPR compliance, cookie consent management, and right to be forgotten implementation.

## Platform Overview for AI Context

**Platform Name:** Ascended Social  
**Business Entity:** Third Eye Cyborg, LLC  
**Platform Type:** Spiritual community and wellness platform with global user base  
**Geographic Scope:** United States (primary), European Union, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia  
**Target Audience:** Adults seeking spiritual growth and community (18+)  
**Jurisdiction:** United States (with international compliance requirements)  
**Privacy Tools:** Enzuzo cookie consent management, PostHog analytics, comprehensive data protection

## Document Requirements

### 1. GDPR Compliance Statement
**Required for:** More detailed privacy compliance than standard privacy policy

### 2. Cookie Consent Management Documentation  
**Required for:** Enzuzo integration procedures and cookie categorization

### 3. Right to be Forgotten Implementation
**Required for:** Data deletion request processes and user data portability

## Primary Document Recommendation

**"Compliance Manual - Financial Institution / Fund"**

This document type is ideal because it:
- Provides comprehensive regulatory compliance framework
- Addresses international data protection requirements
- Supports multi-jurisdictional legal obligations
- Includes detailed procedural documentation for compliance processes
- Covers financial data protection (relevant for subscription billing)

## Document Structure Requirements

### Executive Summary
- Overview of international compliance commitment
- Multi-jurisdictional regulatory acknowledgment
- Data protection principles and user rights recognition
- Cross-border data transfer safeguards

### Regulatory Framework Compliance
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
- UK Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR
- Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) - Canada
- Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APP) - Australia
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA) - United States

### GDPR Compliance Implementation
- Lawful basis for data processing (Article 6)
- Special categories of personal data handling (Article 9)
- Data subject rights implementation (Articles 15-22)
- Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) procedures
- Data Protection Officer (DPO) designation (if required)
- Cross-border data transfer mechanisms (Standard Contractual Clauses)

### Cookie Consent Management (Enzuzo Integration)
- Cookie categorization and classification procedures
- Consent collection and documentation methods
- Consent withdrawal mechanisms and immediate effect implementation
- Cookie scanning and inventory management
- Third-party cookie governance and vendor management
- Technical implementation of consent enforcement

### Right to be Forgotten Implementation
- Data deletion request intake and verification procedures
- Timeline compliance (30 days for GDPR, varying for other jurisdictions)
- Technical deletion processes across all data systems
- Third-party data processor notification requirements
- Data retention exception handling (legal obligations, public interest)
- User notification and confirmation procedures

### Data Subject Rights Procedures
- Right of access (data portability and export)
- Right to rectification (profile correction procedures)
- Right to restrict processing (consent granularity)
- Right to data portability (machine-readable format provision)
- Right to object (automated decision-making and profiling)
- Rights related to automated decision making and profiling

### International Data Transfer Safeguards
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) implementation
- Adequacy decision reliance (where applicable)
- Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs) consideration
- Transfer impact assessments for high-risk jurisdictions
- Data localization requirements compliance

### Third-Party Service Provider Compliance
- PostHog Analytics: GDPR-compliant configuration and data processing agreements
- Stripe Payment Processing: PCI DSS compliance and international payment regulations
- OneSignal Communications: Email delivery and notification consent management
- Cloudflare Services: Data protection and international content delivery
- Enzuzo Cookie Management: Consent technology compliance and vendor agreements

### Breach Notification Procedures
- Incident detection and assessment protocols
- Regulatory notification timelines (72 hours to supervisory authorities)
- Data subject notification requirements and thresholds
- Internal escalation and response team activation
- Documentation and reporting requirements for compliance demonstration

### Compliance Monitoring and Auditing
- Regular compliance assessment schedules
- Internal audit procedures and documentation
- External compliance verification processes
- Regulatory update monitoring and implementation procedures
- Staff training and awareness programs for international compliance

### Updates and Regulatory Changes
- Regulatory change monitoring and assessment procedures
- Policy update implementation timelines and user notification
- Grandfathering provisions for existing user data and consent
- Legal review and approval processes for international compliance updates
